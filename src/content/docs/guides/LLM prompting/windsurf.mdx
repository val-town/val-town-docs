## Instructions

1. Install the [Val Town CLI](https://github.com/val-town/vt) and log in
2. Open [Windsurf](https://windsurf.com/)
3. Copy-paste our [system prompt](https://www.val.town/x/valdottown/Townie/code/prompts/system_prompt.txt) into [`global_rules.md`](https://docs.windsurf.com/windsurf/cascade/memories#rules)
